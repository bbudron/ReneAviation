Sport Pilot

$3300

20 hr of airplane rental @ $105/hr = $2100

15 hr of Instruction @ $60/hr = $900

Books, headset and materials = $300

  • Becoming a Sport Pilot is the quickest, easiest and most economical way to get up in the air. At half the cost of a private pilot certificate a sport pilot certificate allows you to:
  • Fly with up to one passenger
  • No FAA medical required
  • Fly during the daytime
  • Fly anywhere within the continental U.S
  • Fly over the NASA Shuttle Landing Facility where the space shuttle once operated. On your way to the space coast enjoy the scenic view of Orlando, Sanford and other landmarks our instructors will point out along the way. This is a great flight for anyone who loves NASA.
