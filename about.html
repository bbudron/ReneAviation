My introduction to aviation came as a direct result of my wife taking a Discovery Flight. She came home one day and said she flew an airplane with Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) Joey. She went on to say how she enjoyed the entire experience. Several years later, I sought out the services of CFI Joey Castillo. Joey agreed to be my instructor. From the moment we lifted off the runway, I experienced nirvana. It stood to reason that I immediately signed up for Sport Pilot training. Joey and I spent many hours together in the cockpit and exchanged ideas and philosophies. Part of these many discussions revolved around forming a new aviation school in Central Florida. It is no surprise that Joey is now the Chief Pilot for Rene Aviation.
